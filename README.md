## 📚 What is notebooklm-skill?

notebooklm-skill is a tool designed to help you do research and create content without hassle. It combines two main parts: Claude does the research and writes the content, while NotebookLM organizes the information. The process goes like this:

- Research
- Synthesis
- Content Creation
- Publishing

This tool uses Claude Code Skill with MCP Server to automate the pipeline. You get a clear path from finding information to producing finished material.

---

## 🔍 Who is this for?

This app works well for people who want to create content based on research but may not have technical knowledge. You do not need to know code or run complex commands. It fits writers, podcast creators, educators, and anyone involved in content creation.

## 🛠️ How to Use notebooklm-skill

This section explains basic usage once you have the app installed.

### 1. Open notebooklm-skill

Start the app by clicking its icon. The interface is simple and user-friendly.

### 2. Start a Research Project

- Click "New Project."
- Enter a title and description of your research topic.
- Press "Start."

### 3. Claude Does the Research

The system will search for relevant content and organize the data. This process may take a few minutes depending on the topic.

### 4. Review and Edit

Once the research is ready, read through the gathered information. You can add notes or remove anything not needed.

### 5. Generate Content

Use the built-in function to create drafts or scripts based on your research. Claude will write content ready for editing or publishing.

### 6. Export or Publish

Export your finished work as a text file, PDF, or publish directly if the app connects to your platform.
